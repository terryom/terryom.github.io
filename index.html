I'm a third-year PhD student in University College Dublin, where I am supervised by Prof Morgan Kelly. I am also an economist in the Central Bank of Ireland, where I mostly work on household financial stability topics, including mortgage default and modification and non-performing loan resolution. There's me in the centre, between John McHale and John Muellbauer, receiving the 2017 Conniffe prize at the IEA annual conference.

I am also an occasional lecturer in UCD, teaching undergraduate economic policy.
